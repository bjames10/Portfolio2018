Camp Journey - Fact Sheet

April 21st, 2017

Programs used

    Microsoft Word

Camp Journey is a summer camp for youth with cancer, in my public relations class we were assigned to create a mockup of a fact sheet for them.


The difficulty of this project came from the lack of information, there wasn’t much on their website so there is a lot of theoretical information. I started by writing out my content and then designing the rest based upon it. I made revision throughout My information came from the Ross Point site and their Facebook page mostly, I tried to design it similarly to other fact sheets for charity groups that I’d seen used. I am happy with how my project turned out.

RPIE Public Relations Crisis Plan

March 21st, 2017

Programs used

    Microsoft Word

The final was designed to demonstrate my knowledge of RPIE readiness (Research, Planning, Implementation, Evaluation) for public relations crisis. My proposed plan shows my ability to propose ethical and realistic situations to communication based problems.


For the final we were to draft a crisis plan that would demonstrating our knowledge of RPIE. The crisis scenario was a case of E. Coli outbreak at a grocery store and how I would handle it if I were in working in the company’s public relations team. started my plan by researching E. Coli and the how outbreaks were handled by other company’s (Chipotle). I then moved onto researching the legal implications and what agencies would need to be involved. My format is just a simple list in chronological order for a possible campaign.
